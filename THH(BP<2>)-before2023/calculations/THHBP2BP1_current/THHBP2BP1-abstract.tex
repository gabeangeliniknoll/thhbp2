\begin{abstract}
We compute topological Hochschild homology of the second truncated Brown-Peterson spectrum $\B$ at the prime $3$ with coefficients in $\tBP{1}$. We use the model for $\B$ constructed using a Shimura curve of discriminant $14$ due to Hill-Lawson, which an instance of topological automorphic forms.
\end{abstract}
